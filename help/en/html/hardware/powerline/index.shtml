JMRI: Powerline Controllers

(This page talks about how to configure JMRI to talk to powerline controllers, e.g. X10)

Currently we are using the X10 CM11A serial device as our test implementation. Once that is working, we expect to add more of the other interfaces. Eventually the Insteon device family will be supported once we have a developer who buys one of those interfaces.

See also the associated compatibility page to see what interfaces have been supported.
