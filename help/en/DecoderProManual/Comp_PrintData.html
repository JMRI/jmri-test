One of the newest features of DecoderPro is the ability to print out a full list of the decoder CV data. To print, select the "File" item in the top left corner of the pane, then select "Print" and choose "All" or "CVs". "All" will print the full set of information for a decoder using plain language labels for the settings, and "CVs" will print only the numeric list of CVs and their values.
