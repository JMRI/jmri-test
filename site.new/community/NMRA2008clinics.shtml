JMRI Clinics at the NMRA 2008 Anaheim Convention

For those planning to attend the NMRA Anaheim National Convention, July 13th thru July 19th, 2008, six clinics and our annual user meeting have been scheduled. Two of the six clinics will be repeated.

For planning purposes, please note that Sunday night 7PM-11PM and Monday night 7PM-11PM and Wednesday night 10PM - 11PM are the main JMRI clinic times. Starting Sunday night is earlier in the meeting than usual, but these are the best times we could get.

Our JMRI User Group Meeting will be Wednesday night. The two introductory clinics will be presented on Sunday and repeated on Friday.

The following JMRI clinics have been scheduled:

7:00 PM, Sunday, July 13th Intro to Decoder Programming with JMRI/ DecoderPro Dave Duchamp
8:30 PM, Sunday, July 13th Introduction to Layout Control with JMRI/ PanelPro Dick Bronson
10:00 PM, Sunday, July 13th Add Signals to your Layout with JMRI/ PanelPro Dick Bronson
7:00 PM, Monday, July 14th Advanced Decoder Programming with JMRI DecoderPro Bob Jacobsen
