JMRI Clinics

This page contains links to various clinics that have been presented by JMRI users. They've made them available here for your use, but please contact the original author before presenting them yourself.

We have a page of clinics from the 2008 NMRA convention in Anaheim.

Dick Bronson has provided several animated introductions to DecoderPro and PanelPro on his own web pages. The most recent version was presented at the 2007 NMRA convention in Detroit.

Peter Ulvestad of the Edmonton Model Railroad Association has created a nice PDF handout and animated clinics for:

  • Programming a New Decoder
  • Copying a Roster File
  • Making a Manual Roster Entry

Nick Kulp has posted a PanelPro clinic that he presented in 2004.

Keith Albright has posted a clinic on speed matching locomotives using DecoderPro.
