JMRI Defense: Papers from the Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit

Most of our legal defense is happening in the Northern District of Calfornia federal court. Papers from there are available on our main docket page.

On this page, we're providing copies of the court papers from our appeal to the Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit, located in Washington DC.

Court of Appeals Decision

On August 13, 2008, the Court of Appeals ruled in favor of JMRI. The entire ruling is available as a PDF file. This decision can be referenced as "Jacobsen v. Katzer, 535 F.3d 1373, 1381 (Fed. Cir. 2008).".

Note that these are presented in a "logical" order, not necessarily the chronological order in which they were filed. This is to intended to make it easier to understand how the key documents are interrelated. The complete chronological listing is below.
