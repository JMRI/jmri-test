JMRI Defense: Court Papers

Court documents are generally public, except for those filed "under seal". The public documents can be accessed through a system called PACER. PACER provides retrieval and a certain amount of searching, but unfortunately costs $0.08 per page.

As a convenience for people following the case, we're providing the most important documents here, and access to the complete list further down the page.

Key Documents

Note that these are presented in a "logical" order, not necessarily the chronological order in which they were filed. This is to intended to make it easier to understand how the key documents are interrelated. The complete chronological listing is below.

      • Bob Jacobsen's reply, with associated declarations from
        • Bob Jacobsen, along with exhibits and
        • Hans-Rudi Tanner, along with exhibits.
      • Russell's request for judicial notice of actions by the Patent Office.
      • Russell's reply and associated objections to evidence.
      • Jacobsen's response to objections to evidence.
    • Kevin Russell's motion to strike, memorandum of support, declarations by Russell and Matt Katzer.
      • Bob Jacobsen's reply, with associated declarations from
        • Bob Jacobsen, along with exhibits and
        • Hans-Rudi Tanner, along with exhibits.
      • Russell's' request for judicial notice of actions by the Patent Office.
      • Russell's reply and associated objections to evidence.
      • Jacobsen's response to objections to evidence.

      • Jacobsen's objections to the demands for fees by Jerger.

    • Katzer's Second MOTION to Dismiss, Motion to Strike, and Motion for a More Definite Statement and associated declaration by Jerger.
    • Jacobsen's opposition to the motion and associated exhibits.
  • Motion for preliminary injunction regrading copyright violations.
    • This is accompanied by two declarations and a number of exhibits.
